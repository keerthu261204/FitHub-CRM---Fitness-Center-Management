fitHub CRM - Fitness Center Management
A modern Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system designed specifically for fitness centers, gyms, and wellness studios. FitHub CRM helps manage members, trainers, memberships, attendance, payments, and workout schedules efficiently.

📌 Overview

FitHub CRM is built to streamline the daily operations of a fitness center by providing a centralized platform for:

Member registration and profile management
Trainer and staff management
Membership plans and renewals
Attendance tracking
Payment and invoice management
Workout and class scheduling
Reports and analytics

✨ Features
👤 Member Management

Add, update, and delete member details
Store contact information and fitness goals
Track membership status
View attendance history

🏋️ Trainer Management

Manage trainer profiles
Assign trainers to members
Track trainer schedules

💳 Membership & Billing

Create multiple membership plans
Monthly / quarterly / yearly subscriptions
Renewal reminders
Payment history and invoice generation

📅 Attendance & Scheduling

Daily member check-in / check-out
Class booking system
Trainer session scheduling
Slot availability management

📊 Reports & Dashboard

Active vs inactive members
Revenue reports
Membership growth analytics
Attendance statistics

🔮 Future Enhancements

-Mobile app integration -AI-based workout recommendations -Automated reminder notifications -Diet and nutrition tracking -QR code-based attendance
